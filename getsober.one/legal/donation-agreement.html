1.5. The Receiver has the right to cancel the Offer at any time at his own discretion and without giving any reasons. The Receiver has the right to make changes and additions to the Offer, which come into force on the day of their placement on the Website.

2.1 The Giver shall transfer free of charge to the Receiver money in the amount determined by the Giver.

2.2 The Agreement is concluded by accepting the Offer by the Giver. 

2.3 The Offer shall be deemed accepted by the Giver if the Giver has transferred funds (Gift) in favor of the Receiver via the payment form available at: https://www.getsober.one/work-with-us/donate/.
