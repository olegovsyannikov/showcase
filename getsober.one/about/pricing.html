Question and answers hub

Learn more about how Sober One works, if you are still not sure.
Do I have to stop drinking immediately?
No. We don't force anyone to quit at all. It is up to the person to make the choice. First we will analyze the situation. When you are ready to quit, you will choose your own comfortable date to start sobriety.
What if the app won't help me?
We cannot give a one hundred percent guarantee. Overcoming addiction involves a lot of work on the part of the person. But you will definitely gain knowledge and skills. If the app doesn't work for you at all, we'll give you your money back.
How long should I use the app?
We recommend a minimal period of 3 months. Of course, everyone has their own pace. Some people who come to Sober One have strong motivation already. But there are also those who need more time.
Restrictions and security
The online format is not suitable in all cases. If you've tried quitting on your own but couldn't sustain it more than one month, it's better to talk to a therapist offline. Sober One can also be used, but as additional recovery support.
